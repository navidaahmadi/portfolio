What This Looks Like in Practice

Classroom Practices

  • Intentional invitations to play with open‑ended materials
  • Positive guidance and emotion coaching
  • Daily health, safety, and sanitation routines
  • Observation notes, photos, and learning stories

Family Partnerships

  • Warm, consistent communication about learning and routines
  • Respect for home languages and cultural practices
  • Collaborating with professionals on individual plans
  • Sharing resources that extend learning at home
